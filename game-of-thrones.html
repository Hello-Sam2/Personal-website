It's about the world of the Seven Kingdoms, where we follow multiple characters in the story notably the family of Lord Eddard Stark, Lord of Winterfell, and old Kingdom of the North. He has 5 children, Baby Rickson, Arya, who rejects all sorts of royal duties, and her sister Sansa, the model princess. There is also Bran, the second eldest son, and Robb, the heir to Winterfell. There is also Jon Snow, the King's bastard. The story progresses to follow Tyrion Lannister, the imp, and the King of the Seven Kingdoms, Robert Baratheon. Meanwhile, Daenerys, sister to Viserys, the last of the Targareyans who were King of the Seven Kingdoms for hundreds of years until recently deposed, is gaining power in a faraway land. From the way he intricately combines first-person accounts to murder and history; it is much too intricate and awesome to display here. Go read it!
