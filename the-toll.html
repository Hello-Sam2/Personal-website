The Toll

  • Blog post #2
  • Book author: Neal Schusterman
  • Genre: Fantasy

Summary:

In the culmination of a trilogy, all the characters we know and love are seamlessly brought back together. When all hope might be lost, Neal somehow brings us back with a glimmer of hope. The intricate storylines are all separately engaging and developing until the very end where the even the Thunderhead's story-arch is completed. This book surprised me up until the very end from the unexpected twists and turns to the suspenseful chapters.

How I discovered the book:

Afer reading the second book of the series, Thunderhead, and anxiously awaiting the release of this one.

My Thoughts:

The best book in the series hands down, kept me up for hours reading.

What I liked about it?

He somehow found a way to connect all the story lines and tie up all of the loose ends, while not making it feel rushed.

What I didn't like about it?

Again, nothing to see here.

Who would like it?

Any interest in fantasy/AI and future predicting books would be appreciated, but is easily enjoyed without as well.
