I generally wanted to become more extraverted this year, and I think I have made progress on it (I guess you guys can tell me though). It's cerataintly related to confidence (like writing this post), but also focusing less on external motivations.

Maybe I'm generalizing, but when thinking about what others think, you are always less confident. By relinquishing control to other people, whatever you thought you could change is no longer the same. You can control your actions, but once you make your thoughts related to others opinions, you have just moved whatever the thing is out of your locus of control. I know this is quite a tangient, but it's important to me.
