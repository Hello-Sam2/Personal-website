Sam's April 2021
Goal Update

Notes:

Essentially, the main groups on this list are the value goals, and the sub-topics are the goal-outcomes (don't know what that means? Click anywhere here to see my system for goals and try to make your own!).

I am going to add an action point for each outcome, and update on them next month when I hopefully have finished them!

These are my current goals, of both varieties.

‚õ∞Seeking Discomfort

Whatever you want to call this, a mantra, a value, a goal, it's still important. Life just ain't interesting unless you're exiting your comfort zone, and I'm hoping to exert this through all my other goals. In my study of Bruce Lee's philosophy, he references the final stage of actualization, which is being wholly yourself. There is always an emphasis on acting and not second-guessing. Not wasting energy on what might go wrong.

Action point:

When you think to do something, do it. Whether that is washing the dishes or texting someone, it doesn't matter.

I've been focusing especially this month on talking to more people that you might know less well. It's very important to catch up, and hang out with those who you are close with, but it's just important to get to know more people, so you can eventually become closer to them.

Whether this is someone you know slightly, or someone you always see but don't even know their name, it's still good to at least start a conversation, who knows what could happen?

This last month has been quite different from the ones previous. I took a break from all this "self-improvement" stuff, and now my birthday is coming up.
