Thoughts:

Overall, it was some good bread, but not necessarily baguettes. The recipe was pretty good though I made a few mistakes, my technique can always be better.

How I discovered the recipe:

Chef John from Food Wishes.com with...

What I liked about it?

It does give a slightly different crust than a regular bread. If you are not so good at kneading, this is the recipe for you.

What I didn't like about it?

Not much bad about the recipe itself, just remember to trust Chef John, and weight everything in grams if it is shown in the recipe.

Who would like it?

Anyone who has made some bread and wants to make baguettes or a more advanced recipe, this is a good starting point.
