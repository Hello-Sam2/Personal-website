Summary:

It's about American, English, and Australian POW's in a camp in Singapore held by the Japanese. The story centers around the King, an American who is smart with trading, though it is illegal, and Peter Marlowe, an innocent Englishman who fiercely believes in the English Class System. The supporting characters enrich the story with many subplots most importantly through Grey, an English officer keen on ratting out the King for, something. Whether it is through a hidden radio or outside the tall fences, subplots create the suspense of the story!
