The Game of Lives (James Dashner)
Book Review

Summary:

It's about a version with the future with two places. The Wake and the VirtNet. in the VirtNet, you can go to many different places like Lifeblood, close to real life. without the worry of dying. Until a mysterious thing known as Kaine starts taking out things known as cores, which allow you to go into the Sleep. When you die without your core, you die for real. Michael and his friends must find Kaine and attempt to stop him, how to do this? Use your coding skills to manipulate the game to your advantage.

🔎How I discovered the book:

After reading the Maze Runner Series, this series then came out. I had yet to read it until I saw the first two books at a used book store for one buck each.

🧠My Thoughts:

Wow, I don't know how he does it! The endings never cease to amaze me! this one wasa completely crazy and unexpected and truly makes you want to read the rest of the series.

👍What I liked about it?

The ending was incredibly deveiving, it was as if the universe was flipped upside-down!

👎What I didn't like about it?

Especially after reading historical fiction, the writing style can seem a bit childish at times.

🤔Who would like it?

It's a great quick read for anyone who might want a break from heavy stuff.
