The Rule of Thoughts (James Dashner)
Book Review

Summary:

After Michael is transplanted into a human body, he realizes the problems with being mortal. Having to balance finding his friends in the real world, evading the police, and trying to fit into a human being's life that he took over, Michael and his friends have a lot on his plate.

🔎How I discovered the book:

After reading the Eye of Minds, I couldn't wait to find out how Michael would handle his life being turned upside-down.

🧠My Thoughts:

I really liked the book, and there was constant action. The stakes are the highest they have ever been, and I can't wait until I can read the third book!

👍What I liked about it?

The characters are incredibly written, and the suspense makes you never want to put it down.

👎What I didn't like about it?

Nothing to see here.

🤔Who would like it?

Dystopian fiction readers will continue to love this series.
